A Shot for Life

What is A Shot for Life(ASFL)?

A Shot for Life is a Cancer research nonprofit that raises money for Mass General Hospital through a variety of sports events. Every year hundreds of athletes will wear an ASFL jersey and band together to step on the floor, field, or rink for all those who canâ€™t.

My role:

I was given the opportunity to intern for A Shot for Life the summer after my freshman year. During that time I worked on a variety of initiatives including but not limited too:

  • The addition of three new initiatives, The Courage Cup(Soccer), The ASFL Bowl(Football), and the ASFL Classic(Hockey)
  • The addition of the ASFL Pitchers challenge to ASFL Baseball and Softball
  • The addition of the ASFL Basketball All-Star Game
  • Running numerous events including the ASFL Gauntlet, a 24 hour basketball event with over 350 participants

On top of these responsibilities I got to make meaningful connections to many members of the A Shot for Life community. I also got to see first hand that we were making a difference. The money we raised is helping fund groundbreaking trails at MGH. Although my time as an intern is over, my time with ASFL is not. I am still a very active member of the community and help wherever I am needed!
